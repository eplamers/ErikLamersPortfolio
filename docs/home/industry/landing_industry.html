    Hip Exoskeleton

    Providing mechanical assistance to the hips is particularly challenging for a few key reasons:

  • Nearby anatomy is especially squishy (e.g. abdomen, waist)
  • The hip is a 3 degree-of-freedom joint (i.e. ball joint)
    • While at Google X, I led the design of multiple iterations of hip exoskeletons prototypes capable of delivering up to 35Nm of torque and fitting multiple sized users.
